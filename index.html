About

I am an aspiring artist studying Digital Media Art at San Jose State Univeristy. Ever since I got my hands on my first ever game (Pokemon Pearl for the Nintendo DS) I have an ever growing facsination with digital art and coding. I'm the type of person who wants to do it all, whether it be coding, photography, design, video, storytelling etc. and once I get hooked on a certain thing I will try my best to learn every single aspect about it. The more skills the better, hence why I chose Digital Media Art as the major covers such a large variety of areas in the digital world.

Recently I have been delving into the realm of pixel art and coding in order to make my own assets for games and other such projects I decide to pursue in the future. Some of my preffered programs/methods to make art are Photoshop, Illustrator, Java/JavaScript, Aesprite, and AfterEffects with Unity being one of the programs I really want to dive into to start making games myself.

From Below

Next

The underworld across a variety of different cultures and beliefs is often depicted as a place of endless suffering, torture, and insanity. I decided to replicate these aspects of the underworld as an endless top down shooter game where you fend off creatures from below where you can progressively get stronger as you defeat more demons, although an ending may be advertised for the description of this game it only represents a false hope to the player as their is no end if you're sentenced to the underworld. My main inspiration for this game is that of another game titled Realm of the Mad God in which character death is permanent and the repetition of gameplay going through dungeons of various enemies until your inevitable death. It is a gameplay loop that is very addicting for those up to the challenge, a never ending cycle of suffering.

OC Slot Machine

Presented here is my Original Character Slot Machine. I wanted to create something similar to a video game that describes characters that I and my significant other have made in the past. During the creation of this project, I began teaching myself pixel art on Aseprite. So I used this project as a means to practice creating assets for games I would want to make in the future.

Button that randomizes 5 different icons corresponding to a character/item.
Upon getting three of the same icon, text will appear describing the icon and victory music will play. Music plays/pauses by pressing 1 and 2 respectively.

Dapper Avatar

I have always felt like I floated through life going on from one thing to the next and occassionally spiraling out of control from an obstacle here and there. I wanted to represent this same feeling through my avatar.

Features:
Changes color based on mouse position.
Hovering over the center of the avatar causes the avatar to roll rapidly.
Microphone Input causes the mouth and wings to move accordingly.
The left and right arrow keys will change the sky from afternoon to daytime respectively.
The background lines will change color over time.
